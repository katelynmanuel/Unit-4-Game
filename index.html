Crystals Collector

Let's see how many crystals you can collect! You see that Target Number just below? The goal is to use the crystal buttons as many times as you need to reach the value of the Target Number.

At the start of the game, each crystal gets a new number. When you click on that number, you'll see your Current Score increase by the value of the crystal you just clicked. Get to the Target Number to collect crystals, but be careful not to go over or you lose!
